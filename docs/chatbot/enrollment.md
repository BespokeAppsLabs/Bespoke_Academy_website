# Enrollment

## Overview
Enrolling at Bespoke Academy is a straightforward process designed to get students started on their AI and Robotics journey quickly. Our team guides families through each step to ensure a smooth and successful enrollment experience.

## Key Information
- Simple, guided enrollment process
- No placement tests or prerequisites required
- Multiple enrollment periods available
- Personalized onboarding experience
- Complete documentation support
- Flexible start date options

## Common Questions
**Q: How do I start the enrollment process?**
A: Simply contact our admissions team or fill out the online inquiry form to begin your journey with Bespoke Academy.

**Q: Are there prerequisites or placement tests?**
A: No! Our program is designed for complete beginners with no prior experience required.

**Q: When should we enroll?**
A: We recommend enrolling at least 2-3 weeks before your desired start date to ensure proper placement and preparation.

## Detailed Information

### Enrollment Process Steps

1. **Initial Inquiry**
   - Contact our admissions team
   - Discuss program fit and goals
   - Receive detailed information packet

2. **Family Consultation**
   - Personalized meeting with program director
   - Discuss student interests and learning style
   - Answer all family questions

3. **Program Selection**
   - Choose appropriate cohort and schedule
   - Review payment options
   - Understand program expectations

4. **Documentation**
   - Complete enrollment forms
   - Provide necessary student information
   - Review program policies

5. **Onboarding**
   - Student orientation session
   - Technology setup and training
   - Welcome to the Bespoke Academy community

### Enrollment Requirements
- Student age 13-17 (Grades 8-11)
- Completed enrollment forms
- Signed program agreement
- Payment arrangement setup
- Parent/guardian consent

### Timeline and Deadlines
- **Fall Enrollment**: June - August
- **Winter Enrollment**: November - January
- **Spring Enrollment**: March - May
- **Summer Programs**: April - June

### What to Expect After Enrollment
- Welcome packet and resources
- Technology setup assistance
- Parent orientation invitation
- Student readiness assessment
- Community introduction

### Next Steps
Once enrolled, families receive:
- Detailed program calendar
- Technology requirements checklist
- Parent portal access
- Student welcome materials
- Community connection opportunities
