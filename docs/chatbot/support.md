# Support

## Overview
Bespoke Academy provides comprehensive technical and educational support to ensure every student's success. Our dedicated support team is available to help with any questions or challenges that arise.

## Key Information
- Dedicated technical support team
- Multiple contact channels available
- Quick response times guaranteed
- Comprehensive troubleshooting resources
- Parent support and guidance
- Ongoing assistance throughout the program

## Common Questions
**Q: How quickly can we get help with technical issues?**
A: Our support team typically responds within 24 hours, with urgent issues addressed even faster.

**Q: What kind of support do you provide for parents?**
A: We offer parent orientation, progress updates, and dedicated support channels for all family questions.

**Q: Is help available outside of class times?**
A: Yes, we provide support through multiple channels including email, phone, and our online help center.
