# Projects

## Overview
Students at Bespoke Academy develop impressive portfolio projects that demonstrate their skills in AI, robotics, and programming. Each project is designed to build upon previous learning while introducing new concepts and challenges.

## Key Information
- Progressive project difficulty across four phases
- Individual and team-based projects
- Real-world applications and problem-solving
- Portfolio-worthy final projects
- Project showcase and presentation opportunities
- Industry-relevant skill development

## Common Questions
**Q: What kinds of projects will students create?**
A: Students build everything from simple programs to complex AI-powered robots and interactive systems.

**Q: Do students work individually or in teams?**
A: Both! We balance individual skill-building with collaborative team projects to develop communication and teamwork skills.

**Q: Can students keep their projects?**
A: Yes, all projects become part of each student's personal portfolio that they can showcase for college applications or career opportunities.
