# Module 1: AI Foundations & Real-World Applications (Weeks 1-4)

## Overview
Introduction to AI through practical applications across different fields, focusing on hands-on exploration rather than theoretical concepts.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand how to effectively use AI tools for research and content creation
- Develop critical thinking about AI applications in various industries
- Learn to craft effective prompts and manage AI workflows

## Week 1: Introduction to AI Tools & Research
### Hands-on Activities (80% of time)
1. **AI Research Challenge**
   - Using ChatGPT/Claude for academic research
   - Comparing multiple AI sources
   - Fact-checking and verification techniques
   
2. **Practical Exercises**
   - Research a topic of interest using AI
   - Create study notes with AI assistance
   - Build a research workflow

### Mini-Project
Create a research presentation about AI in your favorite industry (music, sports, art, etc.)

## Week 2: Visual AI & Creative Tools
### Hands-on Activities
1. **Image Generation Workshop**
   - Using DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion
   - Prompt crafting for visual content
   - Image editing and enhancement

2. **Creative Projects**
   - Design album covers
   - Create movie posters
   - Generate art for different purposes

### Mini-Project
Design a complete brand package (logo, promotional materials, social media content)

## Week 3: Content Creation & Management
### Hands-on Activities
1. **Content Creation Workshop**
   - Writing with AI assistance
   - Content summarization
   - Translation and localization

2. **Workflow Automation**
   - Setting up content calendars
   - Batch processing with AI
   - Content optimization

### Mini-Project
Create a multi-platform content campaign for a cause you care about

## Week 4: Industry Deep Dives
### Hands-on Activities
1. **Industry Exploration**
   - Healthcare: Medical image analysis
   - Music: AI-assisted composition
   - Law: Document analysis
   - Finance: Market analysis tools

2. **Use Case Development**
   - Identify industry problems
   - Brainstorm AI solutions
   - Create solution prototypes

### Final Project
Develop a proposal for an AI solution in your chosen industry

## Assessment
- Project-based evaluation (70%)
- Peer reviews (20%)
- Documentation (10%)

## Resources
- AI Tool Accounts (ChatGPT, DALL-E, etc.)
- Industry case studies
- Project templates
- Collaboration platforms

*Note: All sessions follow an 80/20 split between hands-on activities and discussion/theory.*
