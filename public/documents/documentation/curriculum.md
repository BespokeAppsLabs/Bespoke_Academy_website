# Curriculum

## Overview
Bespoke Academy's comprehensive 40-week AI and Robotics curriculum is designed for students in Grades 8-11, requiring no prior experience. Our program progresses through four carefully structured phases that build foundational skills and advance to complex applications.

## Key Information
- 40-week program divided into four 10-week phases
- Weekly Friday meetings (2+ hours each session)
- Hands-on project-based learning approach
- No previous experience required
- Ages 13-17 welcome
- Progressive skill development

## Common Questions
**Q: Does my child need prior programming experience?**
A: No! Our curriculum is designed for complete beginners with no prerequisites.

**Q: How much time commitment is required?**
A: Students attend weekly 2+ hour sessions on Fridays, with additional project work between classes.

**Q: What will students learn?**
A: Students progress from digital foundations through electronics, AI concepts, and integrated robotics projects.

## Detailed Information

### Phase 1: Digital Foundations (Weeks 1-10)
Focus on computational thinking, basic programming, and problem-solving skills. Students learn fundamental concepts through engaging projects and exercises.

### Phase 2: Electronics & Robotics (Weeks 11-20)
Introduction to physical computing, circuitry, and basic robotics. Students build their first electronic projects and learn how software interacts with hardware.

### Phase 3: AI Concepts (Weeks 21-30)
Exploration of artificial intelligence fundamentals, machine learning basics, and data analysis. Students work with AI tools and understand their applications.

### Phase 4: Integrated Projects (Weeks 31-40)
Capstone projects that combine all learned skills. Students develop comprehensive robotics and AI projects for their portfolios.
